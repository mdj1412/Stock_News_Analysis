Google has suspended the Chinese shopping app Pinduoduo on its app store after malware was discovered in versions of the app from other sources. Google said in a statement Tuesday, March 21, 2023, that it suspended the Pinduoduo app on the Google Play app store out of “security concerns” and that it was investigating the matter.

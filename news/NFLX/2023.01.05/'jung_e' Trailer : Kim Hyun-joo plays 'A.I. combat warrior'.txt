Netflix is giving a glimpse of the new film Jung_E.The streaming service shared a trailer for the South Korean sci-fi film Thursday.Jung_E is written and directed by Train to Busan director Yeon Sang-ho. The film takes place in "a post-apocalyptic 22nd century, where an A.I. lab leads the effort to end a civil war by cloning the brain of a heroic soldier -- her mother."The trailer introduces Kim Hyun-joo as Jung_E, the "A.I. combat warrior." Late actress Kang Soo-yeon plays Seohyun, a researcher and Jung_E's daughter.Ryu Kyung-soo also stars.Netflix released a teaser trailer for the film in December.Kim and Ryu previously collaborated with Yeon on his Netflix series Hellbound.Jung_E premieres Jan. 20 on Netflix.Copyright 2023 United Press International, Inc.

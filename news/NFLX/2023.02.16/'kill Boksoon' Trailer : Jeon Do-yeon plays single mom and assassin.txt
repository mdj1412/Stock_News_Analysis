Netflix is giving a glimpse of the new film Kill Boksoon.The streaming service shared a trailer for the South Korean action-thriller Wednesday featuring Jeon Do-yeon.Kill Boksoon follows Gil Bok-soon (Jeon), a single mom and expert assassin. The trailer features a conversation between Bok-soon's daughter and one of her work associates."At work, she's a renowned assassin. At home, she's a single mom to a teenage daughter. Killing? That's easy. It's parenting that's the hard part," an official description reads.Sol Kyung-gu, Esom and Koo Kyo-hwan also star.Kill Boksoon is written and directed by Byun Sung-hyun (The Merciless, Kingmaker).The film will have its world premiere at the Berlin Film Festival and start streaming March 31 on Netflix.Copyright 2023 United Press International, Inc.

Netflix is giving a glimpse of The Glory Part 2.The streaming service shared a trailer for new episodes of the South Korean revenge drama Thursday.The Glory follows Moon Dong-eun (Song Hye-kyo), an elementary school teacher who seeks revenge on her high school bullies.The trailer shows Dong-eun (Song) create a "perfect misery" for her former bully Park Yeon-jin (Lim Ji-yeon), who taunts Dong-eun by referencing her past abuse.Lee Do-hyun, Yeom Hye-ran and Park Sung-hoon also star.Netflix also shared a poster for Part 2 featuring the tagline "Welcome to my hell."Netflix previously released a teaser trailer that shows Yeon-jin (Lim) deny doing any wrong to Dong-eun.The Glory Part 2 premieres March 10.The new episodes are part of Netflix's South Korean film and TV slate for 2023.Copyright 2023 United Press International, Inc.

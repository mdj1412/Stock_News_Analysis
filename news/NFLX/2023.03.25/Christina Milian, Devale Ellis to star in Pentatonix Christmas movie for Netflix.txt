Production is set to begin next week on the Netflix romantic-comedy, Meet Me Next Christmas, starring Christina Milian and Devale Ellis."In pursuit of a fairytale romance with the man of her dreams, Layla must race through New York City to get her hands on the hottest ticket in town: the sold out Pentatonix Christmas Eve Concert," the streaming service said in a press release.Rusty Cundieff -- whose credits include Chappelle's Show and White Water -- is directing the project, which was written by Molly Haldeman and Camilla Rubis.The ensemble will also include Kofi Siriboe, Kalen Allen, Scott Hoying, Kirstin Maldonado, Kevin Olusola and Matt Sallee."AHHHHHHH!!

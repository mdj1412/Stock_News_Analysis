

TROY, Mich., Jan. 30, 2023 /PRNewswire/ -- The Editorial Advisory and Securities Review Committee of BetterInvesting Magazine today announced Lululemon Athletica Inc. (NASDAQ: LULU) as its "Stock to Study" and Walgreens Boots Alliance Inc. (NASDAQ: WBA) as its "Undervalued Stock" in the April 2023 issue for investors' informational and educational use.
"Readers of BetterInvesting's magazine and users of its on-line tools for evaluating stocks hold a distinct advantage when deploying their assets in today's market," said Ken Zendel, CEO of the National Association of Investors Corp. (NAIC), the parent organization of BetterInvesting. "Our community of investors and their portfolios are living proof of the value of education, research and basic skills."
Learn more including 6 Steps for Successful Stock Investing at: https://www.betterinvesting.org/learn-about-investing/investor-education/getting-started-with-stocks/6-steps-for-successful-stock-investing
Check the April 2023 issue of BetterInvesting Magazine for more details about the latest stocks. Non-members can utilize the limited, trial version of the BetterInvesting online stock selection and analysis tools to study the investment potential of Lululemon Athletica Inc. and Walgreens Boots Alliance Inc. by viewing their fundamental data and applying judgments.
Committee members are Robert M. Bilkie, Jr., CFA; Daniel J. Boyle, CFA; Marisa Bradbury, CFA; Philip Keating, CFA; Walter J. Kirchberger, CFA; and Anne Nichols, CFA.
As stated, the BetterInvesting committee's Stock to Study and Undervalued Stock choices are for the informational and educational uses of investors. They are not to be considered as endorsed or recommended for purchase by NAIC / BetterInvesting. BetterInvesting urges investors to educate themselves about the stock market so they can make informed decisions about stock purchases.  Investors should conduct their own review and analysis of any company of interest using the Stock Selection Guide before making an investment decision.
About BetterInvesting:

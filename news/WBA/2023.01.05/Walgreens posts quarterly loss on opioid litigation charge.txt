Jan 5 (Reuters) - Drugstore chain Walgreens Boots
Alliance Inc reported a net loss on Thursday, due to a
$6.5 billion opioid litigation charge.Net loss attributable to Walgreens was $3.72 billion, or
$4.31 per share, in the quarter ended Nov. 30, compared with a
profit of $3.58 billion, or $4.13 per share, a year earlier
including a one-time gain of $2.5 billion.
(Reporting by Raghav Mahobe and Aditya Samal in Bengaluru
Editing by Vinay Dwivedi)

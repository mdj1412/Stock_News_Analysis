March 6 (Reuters) - Walgreens Boots Alliance Inc
said on Monday that it plans to dispense abortion pill
Mifepristone in any jurisdiction where it is legally permissible
to do so."Once we are certified by FDA, we will dispense this
medication consistent with federal and state laws," the company
said in a statement.
(Reporting by Akanksha Khushi in Bengaluru; Editing by Sandra
Maler)

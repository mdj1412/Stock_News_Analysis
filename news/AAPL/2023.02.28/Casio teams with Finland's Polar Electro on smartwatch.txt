Casio launched what it called its first smartwatch in 2021 using Google's Wear operating system, entering the wearables market dominated by Apple and Samsung.Casio will add Polar's technology to its newest smartwatch, it said. Polar said it would also offer its algorithms to other commercial partners. 	Financial terms were not disclosed."We have seen a growing global interest from people wanting to better understand their own health and bodies for years now," Takashi Uema, Casio's head of global marketing, said in a statement.

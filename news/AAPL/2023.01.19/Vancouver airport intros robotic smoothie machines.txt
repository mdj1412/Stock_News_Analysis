Travelers visiting Vancouver International Airport can buy smoothies 24/7 at five robotic smoothie machines provided by Burnaby, Canada-based TrendiTech Inc., according to a press release.The machine blends smoothies in about a minute with no added sugars or preservatives before dispensing them into recyclable cups, which include a lid and single paper straw.

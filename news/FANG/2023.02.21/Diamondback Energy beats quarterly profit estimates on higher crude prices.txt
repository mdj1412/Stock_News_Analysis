Feb 21 (Reuters) - U.S. oil and gas producer Diamondback Energy Inc
on Tuesday beat Wall Street expectations for fourth-quarter profit,
helped by higher crude prices due to tighter energy supplies following Russia's
invasion of Ukraine.The Midland, Texas-based company's adjusted net income stood at $5.29 per
share, above analysts' average estimate of $5.22.
(Reporting by Arshreet Singh; Editing by Shinjini Ganguli)

Revenue rose 7.1% to 295.4 billion yuan ($42.8 billion) in the three months ended December, missing analysts' estimates of 296.17 billion yuan, according to Refinitiv data.Parts of China remained under strict lockdown for most of the December quarter, with shoppers holding back on spending amid continued economic uncertainty. JD.com's quarterly net income attributable to ordinary shareholders was 3 billion yuan, compared with a net loss of 5.2 billion yuan a year earlier.Excluding one-off items, the company earned 4.81 yuan per American depositary share.($1 = 6.94 Chinese yuan renminbi) (Reporting by Yuvraj Malik in Bengaluru; Editing by Anil D'Silva)

Feb 13 (Reuters) -U.S. main stock indexes opened higher on Monday as
beaten-down megacap growth stocks gained, while Meta Platforms
climbed on reports of fresh layoffs.The Dow Jones Industrial Average rose 18.12 points,
or 0.05%, at the open to 33,887.39.The S&P 500 opened higher by 6.16 points, or 0.15%,
at 4,096.62, while the Nasdaq Composite gained 40.97
points, or 0.35%, to 11,759.09 at the opening bell.
(Reporting by Johann M Cherian in Bengaluru; Editing by Maju
Samuel)

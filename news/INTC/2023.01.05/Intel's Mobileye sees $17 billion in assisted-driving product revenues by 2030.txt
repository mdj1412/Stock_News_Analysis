Jerusalem, Israel-based Mobileye, which went public in October last year, is a top player in the ADAS market, which shares components with fully automated self-driving systems but costs much less. The company said it will continue testing its autonomous vehicle technology in 2023. Mobileye, which counts BMW, Nissan, Volkswagen, among others as its customers added that it expects the volume of its high-end SuperVision product-based vehicles to reach 1.2 million units in 2026.  (Reporting by Eva Mathews in Bengaluru; Editing by Shailesh Kuber)

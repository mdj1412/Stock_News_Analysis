Feb 15 (Reuters) - U.S. President Joe Biden said on
Wednesday Tesla Inc Chief Executive Elon Musk will open
a "big part" of the electric carmaker's charging network to all
drivers."In building our EV charging network, we have to ensure that
as many chargers work for as many drivers as possible. To that
end, @elonmusk will open a big part of @Tesla's network up to
all drivers. That's a big deal, and it'll make a big
difference," Biden said in a tweet.
(Reporting by Dan Whitcomb and Kanishka Singh)

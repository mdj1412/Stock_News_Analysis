Shares of the company, which supplies to Tesla Inc and General Motors, rose 6% in morning trade.Aptiv is one among the many suppliers increasingly shifting their focus towards electric vehicle businesses as automakers produce more such units.The company said it expects operating income between $3.2 billion and $3.5 billion, compared with the $1.26 billion it reported in 2022.Revenue is estimated to rise to between $23 billion and $24 billion in 2025. Dublin-based Aptiv, which makes advanced driver assistance systems, also forecast global light vehicle production growth of 2% in 2025. (Reporting by Raechel Thankam Job; Editing by Sriraj Kalluvila)

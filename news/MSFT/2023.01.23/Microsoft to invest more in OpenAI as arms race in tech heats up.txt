Jan 23 (Reuters) - Microsoft Corp said on
Monday it would invest more in OpenAI, staking its future on the
startup and technology behind the chatbot sensation ChatGPT and
setting the stage for more competition with its rival Alphabet
Inc's Google."Today, we are announcing the third phase of our long-term
partnership with OpenAI through a multiyear, multibillion dollar
investment to accelerate AI breakthroughs to ensure these
benefits are broadly shared with the world," the software giant
said in a blog post.
(Reporting By Jeffrey Dastin in Palo Alto, Calif.; Additional
reporting by Eva Mathews in Bengaluru; Editing by Krishna
Chandra Eluri)



      Alphabet to Present at the Morgan Stanley Technology, Media and Telecom Conference
    

      MOUNTAIN VIEW, Calif. (February 21, 2023) - Alphabet Inc. (NASDAQ: GOOG, GOOGL) today announced that Ruth Porat, Senior Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Alphabet and Google, will participate in the Morgan Stanley Technology, Media and Telecom Conference. The session is scheduled for 9:10 a.m. Pacific Time / 12:10 p.m. Eastern Time on Wednesday, March 8, 2023.

      About Alphabet Inc.
    

      Larry Page and Sergey Brin founded Google in September 1998. Since then, the company has grown to more than 190,000 employees worldwide, with a wide range of popular products and platforms like Search, Maps, Ads, Gmail, Android, Chrome, Google Cloud and YouTube. In October 2015, Alphabet became the parent holding company of Google.

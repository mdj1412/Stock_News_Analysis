BRUSSELS, March 22 (Reuters) - EU antitrust regulators
are poised to give unconditional approval for Alphabet
unit Google's acquisition of Croatian maths app Photomath,
people familiar with the matter said on Wednesday.Google entered an agreement to acquire Photomath in May last
year. Investors in the Croatian company include Menlo Ventures,
LearnCapital, Goodwater Capital, GSV Ventures and Cherubic.

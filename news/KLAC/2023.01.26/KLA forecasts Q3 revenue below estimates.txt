Jan 26 (Reuters) - U.S. chip toolmaker KLA Corp
on Thursday forecast third-quarter revenue below Wall Street
estimates, sending its shares down about 5.3% after market.The chip manufacturing equipment maker expects third-quarter
revenue between $2.2 billion and $2.5 billion, falling short of
analysts' expectations of $2.55 billion, according to Refinitiv
IBES.
(Reporting by Mrinalika Roy in Bengaluru; Editing by Devika
Syamnath)

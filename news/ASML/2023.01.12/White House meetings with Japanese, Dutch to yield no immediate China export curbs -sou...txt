WASHINGTON, Jan 12 (Reuters) - The White House will
discuss a recent crackdown on exports of chip-making tools to
China with Japanese and Dutch officials during upcoming visits,
but they won't result in "immediate"  pledges from those two
countries to impose similar curbs, a person familiar with U.S.
officials' thinking said on Thursday.
(Reporting by Alexandra Alper; Editing by Leslie Adler)

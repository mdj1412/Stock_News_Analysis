
London shares were down again this morning, partly because several big names, including Barclays and AstraZeneca, traded ex-dividend.
On the plus side, there’ve been some strong corporate results in the past few hours. Nvidia posted better-than-expected revenues and earnings, sending its shares up 9%.
Rolls-Royce jumped 15.1% after it forecast more profit growth in 2023 after 2022 earnings topped expectations.
However, Anglo American fell 2% after cutting its total payout to shareholders by 60%.
